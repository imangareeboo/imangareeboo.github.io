This project was created as part of the DH Practicum run by Dr. Susanna Alles-Torrent at the University of Miami during the Spring 2023 semester. During this semester, students in the class worked on learning how to build sites through GitHub, html coding, applying css codes, and were given advice on how to begin building the projects they had in mind for their own researh/work interests.
